Welcome to Brothers Films Production! We are a boutique Candid Wedding Photography Studio based in Meerut, India and are known for a fun, off-beat and modern approach to creating stories from the best day in your life. Led by Ravi Kumar Gautam & Prince Sagar, we started photographing Indian weddings in 2013, and since then have created hundreds of unique love stories across the world.
